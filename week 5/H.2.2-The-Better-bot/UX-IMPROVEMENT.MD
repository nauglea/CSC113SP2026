# Dr. Cardibotology Improvement Report

## The Single Change
"I changed the terminology used from high level medical speak to lay person’s language."

## Why THIS Change?
This is a problem because many student nurses do not have the knowledge to learn on the level of a cardiologist. Layman's terms helps the student understand and be able to teach their patients the same information.

## Unintended Consequences
The changes lowered the valid responses and increased dramatic effect. The bot really did not help me learn ACS.

## Still Broken (On Purpose)
I wanted to keep the condescending behavior because healthcare providers can have this flaw and students need to know how to deal with it positively. 
The Socratic questioning helps the student learn the whys about ailments. 
I wanted the bot to ask questions that would guide the learner to understand the why. Keeping the dad jokes and puns help students remember by associating humor with facts.

## Lesson Learned
Some improvements are better left to the professionals.
